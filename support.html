Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a transaction?

Tap the "Add" tab at the bottom of the screen. Select your cryptocurrency, enter the amount, choose the date, and tap "Add Transaction".

Where does the price data come from?

CryptoTrackr uses Binance's public API to fetch real-time cryptocurrency prices. Binance is the world's largest crypto exchange, ensuring reliable and accurate pricing data.

Is my data safe?

Yes! All your data is stored locally on your device. We don't have servers or databases - your portfolio information never leaves your phone.

What's the difference between FIFO and LIFO?

FIFO (First In, First Out): Assumes you sell your oldest coins first.
LIFO (Last In, First Out): Assumes you sell your most recent coins first.
Check with your tax advisor which method is appropriate for your jurisdiction.
